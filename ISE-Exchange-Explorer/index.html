Your eligibility position

  • GPAX: 3.90, above the published 2.75 benchmark.
  • Completed study: more than the minimum two semesters.
  • Return rule: return to Chula for at least one semester before graduation.
  • University-level English: IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 79 in the ISE orientation material.
  • Faculty-level English: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL iBT 80, plus interview.

Before choosing a host

  1. Confirm the university appears in the actual Fall 2027 quota round.
  2. Check the last exam or mandatory assessment date, not only the teaching end date.
  3. Confirm enough English-taught ICE/engineering courses are open to exchange students.
  4. Obtain written pre-approval for credit transfer.
  5. Check visa, housing and transcript-processing timing.

How to read the scores

  • Calendar: ability to finish and return before Chula Y4S2.
  • ICE: likely relevance to information, communications, computing and engineering.
  • Travel: ease of regional or cross-border trips during the semester.
  • Eligibility: practical readiness based on your record and known restrictions.

Important limitation

Temperatures are typical long-term ranges, not a 2027 forecast. Historical quotas do not guarantee Fall 2027 availability. Exact host dates and course restrictions can change.

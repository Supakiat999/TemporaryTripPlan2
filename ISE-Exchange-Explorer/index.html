Your eligibility position

  • GPAX: 3.90, above the published 2.75 benchmark.
  • Completed study: more than the minimum two semesters.
  • Return rule: return to Chula for at least one semester before graduation.
  • University-level English: IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 79 in the ISE orientation material.
  • Faculty-level English: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL iBT 80, plus interview.

Before choosing a host

  1. Confirm the university appears in the actual Fall 2027 quota round.
  2. Open the course page and confirm enough English-taught ICE/engineering modules.
  3. Check prerequisites and whether exchange students may enrol.
  4. Check the last exam or mandatory assessment date, not only the teaching end date.
  5. Obtain written pre-approval for credit transfer.

How to use QS rankings

  • QS is useful as one reputation indicator, not a guarantee that the host has suitable ICE modules.
  • A lower-ranked technical school may offer more relevant courses than a higher-ranked comprehensive university.
  • Use the QS column together with calendar, ICE relevance, course availability and travel scores.

Important limitation

Temperatures are typical long-term ranges, not a 2027 forecast. Historical quotas do not guarantee Fall 2027 availability. Course catalogues, exchange access and QS positions can change.

Eligibility, return rules and application checklist

Use this checklist before treating any university as a final choice.

Your current position

  • GPAX: 3.90, above the published 2.75 benchmark.
  • Completed more than the minimum two semesters.
  • Must return to Chula for at least one semester before graduation.
  • University-level English benchmark: IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 79.
  • Faculty-level benchmark: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL iBT 80, plus interview.

Return-risk method

  • Return-safe: completion by 31 Dec 2027.
  • Medium risk: final activity from 1–16 Jan 2028, or exact January timing is not confirmed.
  • High risk: 17 Jan 2028 or later.
  • Check the last mandatory exam, project or attendance date—not only teaching end.

Before applying

  1. Confirm the host appears in the actual Fall 2027 quota round.
  2. Check English-taught courses and exchange access.
  3. Check prerequisites and timetable compatibility.
  4. Get written pre-approval for credit transfer.
  5. Confirm the exact final assessment date.
  6. Check visa, housing, insurance and transcript timing.

Data limitations

  • Temperatures are typical ranges, not a 2027 forecast.
  • Historical quota slots do not guarantee Fall 2027 availability.
  • QS rankings are overall institutional rankings.
  • Airport transfer times are estimates.
  • Host rules and course catalogues can change.
